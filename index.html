Research Overview

Our research group focuses on advancing the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We specialize in developing robust and interpretable AI systems that can tackle complex real-world challenges. Our current research initiatives span several key areas:

  • Deep Learning for Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing
  • Reinforcement Learning and Decision Making Systems
  • Explainable AI and Ethical Machine Learning
  • AI for Healthcare and Scientific Discovery
